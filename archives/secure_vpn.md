# VPN Breakdown 

Based on extensive research into privacy-focused VPN services, here are the most secure options that prioritize user privacy and transparency:

## **Most Trusted Commercial VPN Services**

### **Mullvad VPN** - Maximum Privacy Focus

Mullvad lets you use its VPN with total anonymity - the company doesn’t know your email address and if you pay in Bitcoin or cash, your financial data is also anonymous  . When authorities seized Mullvad’s servers, they found nothing to hand over, proving their no-logs claims .

**Key Privacy Features:**

- All of Mullvad’s open-source projects are available on GitHub 
- Monthly fee is about half that of other premium VPNs ($5.27/month) 
- Uses RAM-only server infrastructure and quantum-resistant tunnels 
- Based in Sweden (outside Five Eyes but within Fourteen Eyes)
- Accepts cash payments by mail for complete anonymity

### **Proton VPN** - Swiss Legal Protection

ProtonVPN was founded by the makers of secure email service ProtonMail in Switzerland, benefiting from Switzerland’s strong data protection laws . Under Swiss law, ProtonVPN can’t fulfill authorities’ requests for user data because no logs exist  .

**Key Privacy Features:**

- All apps are open-source and available on GitHub 
- Has undergone independent security audits by SEC Consult 
- Offers Secure Core routing through multiple servers in privacy-friendly jurisdictions 
- Based in Switzerland (outside intelligence alliances)
- Accepts Bitcoin and cash payments

### **IVPN** - No Personal Information Required

IVPN doesn’t even need your email address or credit card information and generates a unique account number during sign-up instead of using personal details . When you sign up for IVPN, you will not be asked for any personal information and they will not log any personally identifiable information .

**Key Privacy Features:**

- Open-source apps with independently audited no-logs policy 
- Based in Gibraltar, following EU privacy legislation GDPR 
- Has no affiliate program, meaning they don’t rely on paid reviews 
- Accepts cash and cryptocurrency for anonymous payments 

## **Self-Hosted VPN Solutions (Maximum Control)**

For ultimate privacy where you trust no company or government, self-hosting gives you complete control:

### **WireGuard with Algo VPN**

Algo is an open-source project by Trail of Bits that sets up a personal VPN in the cloud using WireGuard and IPsec protocols . Generates configuration files and QR codes for iOS, macOS, Android, and Windows WireGuard clients .

**Benefits:**

- WireGuard utilizes state-of-the-art cryptography and aims to be faster, simpler, and more secure than IPsec or OpenVPN 
- Installs to DigitalOcean, Amazon, Azure, Google Cloud, or your own Ubuntu server 
- Completely open-source and auditable
- You control all server hardware and logs

### **WireGuard Easy (wg-easy)**

WireGuard Easy turns WireGuard into an easy-to-use web application that requires practically no work in a Linux terminal and is manageable via Docker  .

### **Pritunl**

Pritunl is a free open-source enterprise distributed VPN server supporting OpenVPN, IPsec and WireGuard, with all source code publicly available on GitHub .

## **Key Evaluation Criteria**

When choosing a VPN for maximum privacy, prioritize:

1. **Verified No-Logs Policy**: Look for providers that have been independently audited by firms like PricewaterhouseCoopers or Deloitte 
1. **Jurisdiction**: Prefer companies based outside the Five Eyes, Nine Eyes, and 14 Eyes surveillance groups 
1. **Open Source**: Open-source solutions allow you to browse the code to check that there isn’t any secret logging or undisclosed data collection 
1. **Payment Methods**: Anonymous payment options like cash or Monero
1. **RAM-Only Servers**: RAM-only servers mean providers couldn’t collect your data even if they wanted to, since data is continuously wiped clean 
1. **Independent Audits**: Regular third-party security assessments

## **Bottom Line Recommendation**

For **maximum privacy with minimal technical expertise**: **Mullvad VPN** offers the best balance of anonymity, transparency, and ease of use.

For **complete control and technical capability**: **Self-host WireGuard using Algo VPN** on a cloud server you control.

For **Swiss legal protection with features**: **Proton VPN** combines strong privacy laws with advanced security features.

Avoid any VPN that requires extensive personal information, lacks independent audits, or is based in countries with mandatory data retention laws.
