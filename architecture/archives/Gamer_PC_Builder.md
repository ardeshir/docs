#### What are the best resources on the internet to build Gaming PC with the most customization and general parts modularity?

Here are some of the best online resources where you can find extensive information and guides on building a customizable Gaming PC:

1. **PCPartPicker**: This website (https://pcpartpicker.com) allows you to plan out and build your gaming PC, ensuring all chosen components are compatible. You can view build guides, see reviews from other users, and even price check across different online retailers.

2. **Reddit's /r/buildapc**: This community (https://www.reddit.com/r/buildapc/) is filled with users who are experienced in building PCs. You can ask for advice, share your build, and check out other's builds for inspiration.

4. **AnandTech**: This website (https://www.anandtech.com) publishes deep and comprehensive reviews about computer hardware and technology, which would be helpful when selecting components.

5. **Newegg PC Builder**: This tool (https://www.newegg.com/tools/custom-pc-builder/) can help you select the right components for your build and make sure they're compatible. They also provide video tutorials and guides.

6. **Linus Tech Tips Forums**: The community here (https://linustechtips.com/topic/804946-guide-build-methods-and-techniques/) is very well-informed and the forum contains guides and discussions that might help you through the building process. 

7. **PC Gamer PC Builds**: The site (https://www.pcgamer.com/pc-build-guide/) offers guides for different builds and budgets, along with recommendations on hardware combinations.

When building a PC, it is important to consider your budget and what games you want to play. You will also need to decide on the form factor of your case (ATX, mATX, or ITX) and how much cooling you will need. Once you have a good idea of what you need, you can start shopping for parts.

Here are some other things to keep in mind when building a PC:

Make sure all of your parts are compatible with each other.
Don't forget to factor in the cost of extras like a monitor, keyboard, mouse, and operating system.
Be patient and take your time. Building a PC can be a fun and rewarding experience, but it is important to do it right.
