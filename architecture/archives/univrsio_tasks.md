# Univrs.io Tasks | Call to Open Collaboration 

## Breaking down the roadmap,  into actionable tasks,  to organize the work better and make it easier to delegate tasks to a crowd-sourced open source community. 

### Detailed task breakdown:

#### 1. Conduct Thorough Research
    - Task 1.1: Prepare a detailed report on the existing public cloud services provided by major companies and government initiatives.
    - Task 1.2: Analyze current laws and policies related to technology and infrastructure, highlighting potential regulatory challenges and solutions.

#### 2. Develop a Clear Value Proposition
    - Task 2.1: Create a detailed document outlining the economic impact of the public cloud infrastructure.
    - Task 2.2: Develop case studies or scenarios showcasing how this initiative can enhance equity and access.
    - Task 2.3: Write a comprehensive argument for how a US Government-managed cloud can improve national security and data sovereignty.
#### 3. Build a Strong Team
    - Task 3.1: Identify and recruit professionals with experience in cloud infrastructure, government procurement, technology law, and infrastructure startups.
#### 4. Create a Business Plan
    - Task 4.1: Draft the mission and vision statement.
    - Task 4.2: Design a detailed roadmap with key milestones.
    - Task 4.3: Develop a funding strategy, identifying potential sources and methods of funding.
    - Task 4.4: Identify and reach out to potential partners in tech, civil society, academia, and government.
#### 5. Engage Stakeholders & Build Alliances
    - Task 5.1: Develop a list of key government officials to approach.
    - Task 5.2: Identify and reach out to relevant technology organizations for potential partnerships.
    - Task 5.3: Engage with community organizations to garner support.
#### 6. Advocacy and Lobbying
    - Task 6.1: Plan and execute public campaigns through various channels.
    - Task 6.2: Draft policy proposals and white papers for presenting to government officials and policymakers.
#### 7. Pilot Projects and Demonstrations
    - Task 7.1: Design and execute small-scale prototypes or community-driven cloud projects.
    - Task 7.2: Identify and document successful case studies of public cloud access driving innovation and business creation.
#### 8. Fundraising
    - Task 8.1: Identify and apply for relevant government grants and public funding initiatives.
    - Task 8.2: Pitch to potential private investors for funding.
#### 9. Continuous Feedback and Adaptation
    - Task 9.1: Establish channels for regular community engagement and feedback.
    - Task 9.2: Implement an iterative development process to improve the proposal based on feedback and technological advancements.

These tasks can then be further broken down into sub-tasks as needed, and assigned to volunteers or team members based on their skills and interests. It is also important to develop clear guidelines for participation, a process for reviewing and integrating contributions, and a communication system for coordinating efforts and maintaining transparency.
For example, we might use platforms like GitHub for managing the open-source development work, Slack or Discord for communication, and Trello or Jira for task management. We could also look into using platforms like Open Collective, Plurality for transparently managing and distributing funds.

Additionally, it would be beneficial to create a strong community culture that values each contributor's work, encourages collaboration, and promotes learning and growth. Regular meetings or check-ins, recognition of significant contributions, and a supportive and inclusive atmosphere can help foster such a culture.
