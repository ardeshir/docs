# Benchmark Electronics - Oxide

Oxide Computer Company manufactures parts of its “rack computer” in **Rochester, Minnesota**, leveraging the region’s long-standing expertise in computing hardware production[1]. The company has established **deep partnerships with local manufacturing firms** in the area to support their hardware development[1]. 

The **primary manufacturing partner** Oxide uses in Minnesota is **Benchmark Electronics**. Benchmark operates a major facility in Rochester and is recognized for its experience in complex hardware manufacturing, including cloud computing systems[3]. Oxide and Benchmark began their collaboration in early 2020, with Benchmark providing design, prototyping, and manufacturing services for Oxide's cloud rack systems[3]. This partnership allows Oxide to iterate quickly and address fabrication challenges with the support of Benchmark’s established supplier network and manufacturing capabilities[3].

In summary: 
- **Oxide’s “rack computer” is manufactured in Rochester, Minnesota**[1].
- **Benchmark Electronics** is the contract manufacturer in Rochester building Oxide’s cloud rack systems[3].
